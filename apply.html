LOAN AGREEMENT

Between Invar Chrysos (the Lender) and the Borrower

1. Loan Details
The Lender agrees to advance the Principal Amount. Disbursement shall occur within 24 hours.

2. Interest
Flat interest of 20% on the Principal Amount for the Agreed Period.

3. Repayment & Penalty Interest
Full repayment by the end of the Agreed Period. Overdue amounts attract penalty interest at 5–10% per month.

4. Formation of Agreement
Binding upon signature or permission to transfer funds.

5. Borrower Warranties
All information is true. Funds will not be used for illegal purposes.

6. AML / FIA Compliance
Borrower consents to due diligence and reporting as required by law.

7. Events of Default & Recovery
Lender may accelerate and recover all costs upon default.

8. Governing Law
